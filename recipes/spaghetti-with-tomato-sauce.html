Sauced spaghetti

A classic pasta dish made with spaghetti and a quick homemade tomato sauce — simple, tasty, and comforting.

Ingredients
  • 200g (about 2 cups) spaghetti
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (400g) crushed tomatoes
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tsp dried oregano or basil (optional)
Steps
  1. Boil spaghetti in salted water until tender.
  2. Heat oil, sauté garlic for 30 seconds.
  3. Add tomatoes, salt, pepper, and herbs. Simmer 10 mins.
  4. Drain spaghetti, mix with sauce.
  5. Serve hot.
