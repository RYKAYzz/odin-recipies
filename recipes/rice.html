Rice

Fluffy, tender rice cooked by simmering rinsed grains in water until soft and perfectly steamed.

Ingredients
  • T1 cup white rice
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 tablespoon oil or butter
  • T1/2 teaspoon salt
Steps
  1. Rinse 1 cup rice until water runs clear.
  2. Heat 1 tbsp oil or butter in a pot.
  3. Add rice, stir for 1 minute.
  4. Pour in 2 cups water and a pinch of salt.
  5. Bring to boil, then cover and simmer on low for 15â€“18 mins.
  6. Remove from heat, let sit 5 mins (covered).
  7. Fluff with a fork and serve.
